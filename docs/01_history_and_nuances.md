# 1. History & Nuances

Welcome to your first lesson in Tamil! 

Tamil is one of the longest-surviving classical languages in the world. Learning just a little bit of Tamil—**konjam Tamil**—will go a long way in navigating Chennai.

## Pronunciation Basics

Tamil pronunciation has some unique sounds, particularly its 'L' and 'R' variations. 
Take your time and practice with the daily words!
