# Prompt for Gemini Deep Research

**Context:**
I am building a website called "Konjam" (meaning "a little" in Tamil), which is dedicated to teaching spoken, colloquial Tamil (specifically the Chennai dialect/slang) to newcomers. The goal is to provide practical, everyday language skills rather than formal, written/classical Tamil. 

**Task:**
I have attached 10 markdown files that represent the core 10 lessons of the curriculum. Currently, these files are just brief skeletons containing basic vocabulary and concepts. I need you to act as an expert linguist and local Chennai resident, and conduct deep research to expand upon these 10 topics. 

Your objective is to generate a single, highly detailed, comprehensive "Master Knowledge Reference Base" document (please provide it as a downloadable PDF or a very extensive markdown file).

**Instructions for the Master Reference Base:**
1. **Cover All 10 Topics:** Strictly follow the thematic structure of the 10 attached markdown files (History & Nuances, Survival & Emergencies, Social Interactions, Time & Numbers, Verbs & Grammar, Adjectives, Slang, Travel & Conflict, Everyday Logistics, Household).
2. **Deep Enhancement & Expansion:** For every single topic, drastically expand the content:
   - Provide extensive vocabulary lists relevant to the topic.
   - Include realistic conversational examples, dialogues, and common phrases used in everyday Chennai life.
   - Explain the grammatical rules intuitively (without getting bogged down in formal linguistics).
   - Add cultural context, etiquette tips, and explain the nuances of when to use specific words (e.g., respectful vs. casual tone, adding "-nga" for respect).
   - Incorporate "Tanglish" (Tamil + English) as it is naturally spoken in the city.
3. **Pronunciation Guide:** Every Tamil word or phrase MUST have a phonetic pronunciation guide (e.g., ⟨VEH-noom⟩) and English translation.
4. **Agent-Ready Structure:** This master document will be used as a "ground truth" reference by an autonomous AI coding agent. The agent will read this single file to completely rewrite, expand, and generate the ultimate Markdown files for my website. Therefore, structure the document logically with clear headings, tables for vocabulary, and distinct sections for each of the 10 lessons so the agent can easily parse it.

**Output:**
A massively detailed, structured, and culturally accurate reference guide that contains everything there is to know about these 10 topics in the context of spoken Chennai Tamil.
